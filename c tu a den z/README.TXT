Do thoi gian co han, nen co mot so chuong trinh nguon bai tap khong duoc
dua vao day, ma se duoc dua vao trong lan tai ban ke tiep, mong cac ban 
thong cam.

Trong thu muc LTHT co thu muc ADDON chua cac tap tin nguon suu tam, cac
ban co the dung no de tham khao them ky thuat lap trinh.
